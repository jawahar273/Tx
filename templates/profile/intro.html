Tx

Tx is an umbrella package with compressed of Tx(based on Sanic ) and Raven Bot NLU Bot( Snip-nlu ). This project design in intent for managing daily task with automation and having of conversation in mocking of assistance such JAVIS.

Raven-Bot is design and can be configure for complex task(work in progress) such todolist, reminder jobs, for file uploading etc.

Demo (Profile details)

Right now this is aDemo v0.1.0pre-Alpha stage, Raven-Bot can response to the general question asked in interview section(General introduction by the candidates). Some of the basic question are Tell me about yourself. , Tell me about your certificate. (or the current certification you have. , Tell about your final year project. which all gives you same details related to my certifications ). The question you might ask to Raven-Bot are about me, my certifications, my final year project, open source projects, relocation (these are some of related request right now Raven-Bot can response) .

How to ask the questions to the Raven-Bot?


Just type the question in the brower's address bar with forware slash(`/`) as a prefix ( or just add the `/` before the start of the question). For example click about link for better understanding.
