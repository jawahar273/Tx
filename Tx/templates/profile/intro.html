Tx Project

Tx is an umberall package which intern compressed of Tx(based on Sanic web framework) and TxBot(NLU Bot based on Snip-nlu).

TxBot can be configure for complex task(work in progress) such todolist, reminder jobs, for file uploading etc.

Demo (Profile details)

Right now TxBot can response to the general question asked in interview section(Profile Details). Some of the basic question are Tell me about yourself. , Tell about your final year project. (or the current certification you have. , Tell about your final year project. which all gives you same details related to my cerfitications ). The question you might ask to TxBot are about me, my cerfitications, my final year project, open source projects, relocation (response limit for now) .

How to ask the questions?


Just type the question in the brower's address bar with forware slash as a prefix (`/` before the start of the question). For example click about link for better understanding.
